Random Article

A fighter jet is a military aircraft designed primarily for air-to-air combat to establish air superiority, although many modern fighters also perform ground attack missions. These high-performance aircraft feature advanced engines, sophisticated composite materials, and incorporate specialized features like stealth technology and supercruise capability. Fighters carry various weapons, including missiles, bombs, and guns, and are characterized by high maneuverability and a high thrust-to-weight ratio.
